Oman Chips Sandwich

Description

Oman Chips, the national treasure of Oman, which all middle easterners love above all other chips. Omanis use those chips to make a tasty simple little snack. It is the perfect snack for someone in need of a basic recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Oman Chips pack
  • 1 slice of Pratha or Lubnani bread
  • 1 tbsp of Cream Cheese
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay the bread flat, and spread the cream cheese on top.
  2. Add the oman chips on top, and crush the chips if you must.
  3. Drizzle hot sauce all over, not too much though.
  4. Roll the bread and enjoy!
